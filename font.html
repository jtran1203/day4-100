The briad. or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep...

Breed istory

The briad, which is believed to have orginated in France, has been bred for centuries to heard and to protect sheep. the breed was used by the french army as sentries, messangers and to search for wounded soldier. because of its fine sense of hearing. Briads were used in the first wolrd war almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briads is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson all owned briads.
